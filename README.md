#  CRM Application for Wholesale Rice Mill

A custom-built **Customer Relationship Management (CRM)** system designed specifically for wholesale rice mills to manage operations like customer orders, inventory, invoicing, supplier tracking, and delivery logistics ‚Äî all in one platform.

---

##  Key Features

-  **Customer & Supplier Management**
-  **Product Inventory & Stock Tracking**
-  **Order Booking & Invoice Generation**
-  **Delivery Scheduling & Tracking**
-  **Payment Status and History**
-  **Sales Reports & Analytics**
-  **Role-Based Access (Admin, Sales, Manager)**
